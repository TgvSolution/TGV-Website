## Everything you need to protect your clients

SecurFich is the electronic document management (EDM) solution built for accounting firms, CPAs, and Quebec professionals who want to offer secure file transfer to their clients.

### Bill 25 Compliant

Required

Built-in Quebec Bill 25 compliance. Automatic audit logs, documented consents, retention policies, and secure deletion — everything in place for your compliance.

### Full White-Label Branding

White Label

Your logo, your colors, your identity. Each client accesses a portal branded to your organization — from the login page to notification emails.

### Document Lifecycle

Automated

Manage the full lifecycle of every document: upload, folder organization, configurable retention period, and automatic deletion at expiry. Compliant with retention requirements.

### Built-in Antivirus

Secured

Every file is automatically scanned before becoming accessible. Protection against malware and corrupted files to secure your organization and your clients.

### Consent Tracking

Bill 25

Document and track your clients' consents in compliance with Bill 25. Complete history, timestamps, and verifiable proof for every consent collected.
